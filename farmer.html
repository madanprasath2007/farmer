The Abstract

"Small and marginal farmers face a massive information-to-income gap. Our ecosystem handholds farmers from planting to profit, using Edge-AI for diagnosis and a transparent digital auction to eliminate middlemen."

The Four Pillars

Integrating high-tech AI into simple rural workflows.

The Product

A voice-driven dashboard providing end-to-end guidance. Integrates real-time weather alerts and market prices from AGMARKNET.

AI Diagnosis

An Edge-AI system that identifies crop diseases instantly via photos. Provides offline treatment steps to reduce chemical waste.

Smart Auction

A live bidding platform where wholesalers compete directly. Bypasses commission agents to capture 100% market price.

AI Verification

Computer vision grades harvest quality automatically. This "Verified" digital certificate builds trust and boosts final bidding value.

How it addresses the problem

Eliminating Middlemen

Removes agents taking 10-20% commission, returning full profits to farmers.

Preventing Yield Loss

Early AI detection cuts down the 25% annual loss from late disease discovery.

Voice-First Accessibility

Using Bhashini AI to allow non-literate farmers to navigate via voice in 22+ dialects.

Innovation Highlight

"Unlike typical apps, this system links **AI-verified quality** directly to a **sale price**, creating an objective trust loop between remote buyers and marginal farmers."

Impact Goal

30%+ Increase in Farmer Income

Potential Impact & Benefits

Social, Economic, and Environmental Transformation.

Social Inclusion

Brings 86% of marginal farmers into the digital economy through radical voice-first accessibility.

Environmental Health

Precise AI-guided spraying reduces soil toxicity and chemical runoff into groundwater.

Economic Wealth

Ensures money stays in the village rather than with commission agents, boosting rural liquidity.
